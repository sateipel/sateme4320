Syllabus

Course Description

This course is designed to prepare you for professional life after college by giving you the knowledge and tools needed to confidently navigate the job search process. From building a strong resume to interviewing successfully, the goal is to help you put your best foot forward and stand out to employers.

Throughout the course, you will learn practical skills, strategies, and resources that will help you create a strong job application and find opportunities that align with your career goals.

Goals and Objectives

This course is designed to build your confidence and understanding of the professional world. You will gain practical experience with resources and materials that you can continue using long after the course ends, whether you're applying for internships, full-time jobs, or preparing for interviews.

In addition to course content, you'll hear tips and advice inspired by real professionals and industry experiences to help you stand out in today's job market. You'll also have the opportunity to learn from your classmates, connect with peers who are going through the same process, and build relationships that may benefit you throughout your career.

By the end of this course, you should be able to:

  • Create a polished resume and professional online presence.
  • Identify and apply to jobs that match your interests and qualifications.
  • Develop stronger interviewing and networking skills.
  • Feel more confident throughout the job application process and present yourself as a competitive candidate.

Methods of Evaluation

Throughout this course, you will complete a variety of assignments that are designed to prepare you for the real job application process. Because these assignments are based on real-world professional skills, you are encouraged to complete them as if you were submitting them to a potential employer in order to get the most value from the course.

  • Assignments: 80% of your final grade
  • Final Assessment: 20% of your final grade

The final assessment will simulate a complete job application process, allowing you to apply everything you've learned throughout the course.

Optional resources and supplemental materials are available to provide additional support and practice but will not be included in your final grade.

Course Schedule

Each module will span approximately two weeks, giving you time to complete assignments and engage with the course material. The final week of the course will be dedicated to the comprehensive final assessment.
